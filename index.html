about

Katherine is a Full Stack Developer based in Denver, CO. She works primarily in the MERN stack but is constantly learning, improving, and seeking out new challenges. Katherine is passionate about sleek and intuitive UI/UX and elegant, stream-lined back-end code. Other than her passion for MERN and Material Design, her coding hobbies include learning more about AI, Machine Learning, and Augmented Reality. When not at her computer, Katherine can be found gardening, reading the latest Brandon Sanderson or George RR Martin novel*, or buying anything with llamas on it.

*And Pat Rothfuss, if he would finish the third Kingkiller Chronicle
